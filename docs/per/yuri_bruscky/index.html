Yuri Bruscky

Brasil

(Recife/PE, 1985). Artista sonoro e investigador, doctorando en Sociología de la Universidad Federal de Pernambuco/UFPE. Magíster en Comunicación de la misma institución. Desarrolla investigaciones artísticas explorando intersecciones entre ruido, lenguaje, mediaciones tecnológicas y prácticas cotidianas. Desde 2010 mantiene el sello/productora Estranhas Ocupações, a través del cual edita discos, publicaciones y organiza performances. Cocreador del festival de arte sonoro Rumor y del programa de seminarios y residencias artísticas (Entre) Lugares Sonoros. Coautor del libro Historia de la poesía visual brasileña.

(Recife/PE, 1985). Sound artist and researcher, teaching in Sociology at the Federal University of Pernambuco/UFPE. Master in Communication for the same institution. He conducts artistic research exploring intersections between noise, language, technological media and everyday practices. He has maintained, since 2010, his own/producer Estranhas Ocupações, through which he releases albums, publishes and organizes performances. Co-creator of the Rumor sound art festival and the seminar and artistic residency program (Entre) Sound Places. Co-author of the book History of Brazilian Visual Poetry.
